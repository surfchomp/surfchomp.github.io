About

    What is surfchomp:

    SurfChomp is a personal project made by surfers, for surfers. It gives you live views of beaches through webcams. Whether you're new to surfing or a seasoned pro, SurfChomp has a bunch of surf spots in New England for you to check out. It's easy to use and shows you the best waves. From early mornings to evening sessions, SurfChomp keeps you connected to the ocean's vibe.

    *Disclaimer*

    We do not own any webcams, all webcams are found on local surfing websites & youtube then compiled here!
